Language / Dialect System

Creators could publish original languages, dialects, alphabets, pronunciation guides, slang, and translated documents.

Example Word

“Eshra” — a Meridian word meaning “a memory that does not belong to you.”

Community Proposals

Instead of normal comments, users can submit respectful proposals for world additions. The creator can review, accept, reject, or discuss them.

Example Proposal:

“Could there be a coastal city where people tattoo maps onto their skin so they do not forget disappearing locations?”
