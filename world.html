Static Earth

Static Earth is a grounded psychological crime-thriller world set in a version of modern Earth where the most frightening monsters are people who know how to look normal.

Psychological Thriller Crime Modern Setting 18+ Violence

World Overview

This world focuses on reputation, manipulation, hidden cruelty, and the difference between how people appear in public versus who they are in private. There are no supernatural elements. The horror comes from social trust, charm, and the danger of someone being accepted by everyone while hiding something violent underneath.

Main Character

Nyx is known publicly as charismatic, intelligent, and trustworthy. Privately, he views certain people as disposable once they frustrate, amuse, or disappoint him.

Content Rating

This world is intended for mature audiences. It contains themes of violence, manipulation, murder, sadism, blood, and psychological cruelty.
