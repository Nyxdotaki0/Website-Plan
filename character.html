Nyx

A charismatic, smug, and dangerous character from the world of Static Earth.

Main Character Psychological Thriller Manipulative 18+

Public Reputation

Nyx is seen by others as charming, intelligent, and easy to trust. He has built a reputation as someone reliable and likable, which makes it difficult for people to suspect what he hides.

Private Nature

Beneath his public image, Nyx carries a deep hatred toward people. As he grew older and spoke with more adults and peers, he became more disgusted by what he saw as stupidity, weakness, and hypocrisy.

He does not show this hatred openly. Instead, he masks it behind confidence, humor, charm, and arrogance.

Personality

Nyx is sadistic, smug, stuck-up, and extremely charismatic. He enjoys control and sees certain people as toys once they upset him or catch his interest.

Story Role

Nyx functions as the central figure of the story. The tension comes from the gap between how safe he appears and how dangerous he actually is.
